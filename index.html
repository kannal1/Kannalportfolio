The journey
01

I started in code

My first roles were in software, testing systems and then building them as an ASP.NET developer at Bigdbiz. I learned how products are really made, and where they quietly fail the people using them.

02

Curiosity pulled me upstream

I kept asking why we were building what we built, and for whom. The questions upstream of the code, research, strategy, the experience itself, pulled me toward design.

03

I learned UX properly

B2B design work at Fibonalabs taught me research, systems thinking, and how to design for complex, real-world operational workflows.

04

Now I build products

At Compound I design fintech and wealth products end to end, an engineer who designs, fluent on both sides of the handoff.
