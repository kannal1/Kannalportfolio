Play & Motion

A few things I've made for the joy of making them. Motion design, game UI concepts, micro-interactions. Some grew out of a Dribbble brief, some out of a slow afternoon. None of these are client work, these are where I push the visual and animation craft separately from the structured product surfaces.

Looping
Piece · 01

Connected Car Cluster

Automotive UI · Motion concept

A cinematic reveal of a digital instrument cluster, speedometer, navigation, RPM, and ambient telemetry composed in a unified gauge form. The dual-circle layout is anchored by a perspective grid that pulls the cluster into the cabin space. Camera reveal, UI animation, and dial mechanics built in After Effects with screen designs prepared in Figma.

After Effects Figma 2024

Piece · 02

Mountains, Biome Selection

Casual game · UI concept

A biome selection screen for a casual mobile game concept, five mountain ecosystems represented as floating islands (ice, lava, water, sandstone, organic). The transition between the map view and each individual biome detail carries the personality of each world. Each biome has its own color, illustration style, and animation curve.

After Effects Figma 2024

Piece · 03

Dynamic Island Pay

Micro-interaction · Motion study

A motion exploration of iPhone Dynamic Island handling a transactional moment. The notification glows in from the device chrome, expands to reveal a Google Pay transaction with product detail, and contracts to a confirmation state. A study in how to make a system notification feel premium without feeling intrusive.

After Effects Figma 2024
