01 / THE CHALLENGE

Serious money raises the bar.

Accredited investors look closely, and a $10,000 minimum makes every screen matter more. The product had to feel trustworthy enough for real money while staying as easy to read as a everyday app.

That was the balance on every flow. It had to feel precise without showing off, and premium without ever being confusing.

VISUAL SYSTEM

A quiet palette, a tight type scale, and components built to feel exact. The system did the heavy lifting so the screens could stay calm.

02 / PROCESS
STEP 01

Structure the journey

Accreditation, bank connection, customizing the investment, buying, redeeming. I mapped the full flow first so a high-stakes journey never hit a dead end or a step nobody explained.

STEP 02

Wireframe the stakes

From rough to final, every screen had to earn its place. Wherever a money decision happened, the layout slowed down and explained itself.

STEP 03

Build the brand in

A new gold-forward identity, kept restrained. The premium feel comes from precision and space, not decoration.

Wireframes to final UI. The premium feel gets decided in the spacing, long before any color lands.

03 / THE PRODUCT

Every screen, calm and exact.

The dashboard opens with the numbers that matter and a clear picture of where the money sits. Buying, the calculator, deposits, and redemption all follow the same rule. Explain first, then ask.

Bank linking and accreditation are the heaviest trust moments, so I designed them to feel like reassurance instead of friction.

THE DASHBOARD

Portfolio value and accrued interest lead, then holdings, the interest-rate options, and recent activity. The whole picture in one calm scroll.
